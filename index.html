deckster is a tool for generative skateboard design.*

  1. Hit NEW DECK to generate a new board
  2. HOVER over the blank or board to see dimensions
  3. FLIP, SMOOTH, or DRAG points around with your mouse to refine your new shape.
  4. Change some parameters and SHARE your one-of-a-kind deck with a friend!

* skateboard press not included :(
